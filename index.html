Lets Go

◙ What is Frontend development?

Front-end development is the practice of converting data to a graphical interface.

◙ Who is Frontend developer?

Front-end developer converting data to a graphical interface, through the use of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, etc, so that users can view and interact with that data.

◙ What were the goals of the Hyper Island front-end developer course up to this assessment?
